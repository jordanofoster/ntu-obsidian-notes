# Information

- Does not only include *digital [[Assets|assets]]*  in databases:
	- Removable media ([[Malware#Routes of Infection|If allowed]])
	- Traditional paper documents
	- Knowledge held by staff members
		- Particularly *business-critical* information
- Either taken from *another source*
	- Customer details
	- Bought datasets
- Or *created from scratch*
	- i.e. companies that focus on *research and development*
